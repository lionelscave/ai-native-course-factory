# Module 01 — AI-Native Mindset: From Tools to Operating Systems

## Executive outcome
Learners understand why AI-native transformation requires operating-model change, not isolated tool adoption.

## Lessons
1. Why AI tools fail to transform companies by themselves
2. The AI-native operating model: workflows, agents, memory, governance
3. The control plane as the management layer for AI labor
4. Case study: one person operating with a command center

AI-native maturity map.
