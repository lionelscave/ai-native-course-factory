# Module 02 — AI-Native Economics

## Executive outcome
Learners can explain and calculate how AI changes the economics of intelligence, action, coordination, and governance.

## Lessons
1. Marginal cost of intelligence
2. Marginal cost of action
3. Workflow compression and cycle-time economics
4. Agent labor portfolio: cost, quality, risk, and throughput
5. Measuring ROI beyond productivity theater
